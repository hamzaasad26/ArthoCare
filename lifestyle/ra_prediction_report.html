EDA Highlights

Demographics:

  • Females had higher RA prevalence (~7.4%) than males (~5.2%).
  • Older age groups (50+) show increased RA proportion.
  • Former smokers and lower education groups had higher RA rates.

Numeric patterns:

  • Age and BRI showed the strongest numeric correlation with RA.
  • Dietary variables (calories, fiber, protein) had small but meaningful signals.

Feature groups

Lifestyle (diet, activity), Demographics (age, sex, race), Comorbidities (hypertension, diabetes, hyperlipidemia), Derived features (Age×Smoking).
